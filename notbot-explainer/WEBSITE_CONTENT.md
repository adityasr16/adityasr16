## How NotBot Works
Five signals. One score.
No single check proves you're human. NotBot stacks five lightweight signals that together are economically unviable to fake at scale. Under 2 minutes for a real person.

### SIGNAL 01 — Link Your Accounts
Connect 2+ existing accounts — LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, Google. Not to harvest data — to verify a real digital footprint exists. A 3-year-old LinkedIn with real connections is almost impossible to fake at scale.

### SIGNAL 02 — Phone Verification
One phone number, one account. Raises the cost of fake accounts from $0 to $1-5 each. At scale, that cost kills bot farms. Simple but effective as a baseline layer.

### SIGNAL 03 — Liveness Check
A quick video check — turn your head, hold up fingers. AI confirms a real face in real time. No ID scanned, no biometrics stored. Just a "yes, real human" flag. Kills bot farms that need a unique face per account.

### SIGNAL 04 — Social Vouching
Real humans vouch for each other. Each person can only vouch for a limited number of people — creating scarcity and accountability. Vouches are weighted by the voucher's own trust score. The network gets stronger with every user.

### SIGNAL 05 — Behavioral Consistency
Passive signals confirm you're human over time. Login patterns, device consistency, interaction rhythms. No surveillance — just ongoing proof that a real person is behind the account. Catches compromised or sold accounts.
